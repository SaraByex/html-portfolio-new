The Best Movies According to Sara

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pretty Woman

This is my love story. I will watch it any day any time. Dive into the Julia Roberts & Richard Gere movie.

The age of Adeline

A woman miraculously remained a youthful 29 years of age for nearly eight decades, never allowing herself to get close to anyone lest they discover her secret.

The Intern

A retired man took up a new job - a difficult challenge. Looking to get back into the game, 70-year-old widower seizes the opportunity to become a senior intern at an online fashion site. Ben soon becomes popular with his younger co-worker
